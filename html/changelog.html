07 March 2021

ArcaneMusic updated:

  • There are now space tourists from south of the border. Andele!
  • The two taco recipes are now distinguished by name in game to help tell the two apart.
  • The Restaurant Holoseat Creator now uses a slightly different sprite to help it stand out.

Dennok, Fikou, Armhulen updated:

  • Fixes shuttle decals not working

Fikou updated:

  • Buildmode now respects your dir for turfs
  • adds the jumpsuit variants of skirts to the curadrobe

Fox McCloud updated:

  • His Grace now sounds like a toolbox when picked up/set down
  • Fixes His Grace in-hand icons not updating when he ascends
  • Windup toolbox has more *immersive latch snapping toy action™️*

Ghilker updated:

  • fixed malf AI overload ability from blowing up non-machine atmos devices (like pipes) and the HFR

Ghommie updated:

  • Hieroclub teleportation now respects unteleportable mobs such as anchored AIs and megafauna.
  • Space Dragons are now actually capable of flight.
  • Fixed the E/W sprites for durathread golems' legs being a couple pixels off.

Paxilmaniac updated:

  • The slightly pixel shifted decal next to chemistry is no longer pixel shifted
  • The several windows that spawned on top of walls in cargo no longer do this
  • Directional subtypes to common wall mounts for mapper use

Qustinnus updated:

  • If you get qdeleted while crystalizing as ethereal, you no longer get super-owned
  • makes the game screen bigger by removing the status bar and moving the chat bar

Randomguy523 updated:

  • pAIs can now be emagged

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • The foam finger rocket launcher will no longer make 5000 rockets and freeze the server when spawned
  • Dogs now have all-new AI! Go throw a grenade at Ian and see what happens!
  • Added AI datum support for complex JPS pathing

SgtHunk updated:

  • Kilostation's security office is no longer haunted by a red siding in one of the security office's tiles.
  • The snowman head's name is now full lowercase.

Sparkezel updated:

  • Veil fluctations returned to normal values, cult can once more place runes in maintenance and few other areas and blob has lost its aversion to books.

StoneMason / Dwight Walker updated:

  • Chav is now closer to being an actual Chav

Thalpy updated:

  • fixes organ exploits with impure libitoil and convermol
  • Improves the chemical analyser to give you details on impurity and gives an % output in line with other purity descriptions
  • fixes Multiver's purity effects

Timberpoes updated:

  • Fix bug that prevented admins toggling playtime exempt status on accounts.
  • Agent ID cards no longer wipe their own access when Reset.
  • Comms Consoles now have the ability to request codes to the spare ID safe when there are no crew members in the chain of command on the shift. This can be done without logging into the comms console.
  • Centcom ID consoles have extended template support.
  • ID cards with no registered age will no longer show null age in Plexagon Access Management.

Zytolg updated:

  • Wall/Floor Lattice cleanup.

carshalash updated:

  • Carbonation

maxymax13 updated:
